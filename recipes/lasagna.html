Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish made with layers of pasta, meat, and cheese.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground beef
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Marinara sauce
  • Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook the lasagna noodles according to package instructions.
  3. Brown the ground beef in a skillet.
  4. Layer noodles, meat, cheese, and sauce in a baking dish.
  5. Bake for 45 minutes until bubbly.
